Common FAQs

What is Tradiction?

Tradiction is a site that is catered to help you make the best financial decisions in the stock market. We work to keep your experience hassle-free by serving as a one-stop shop where you can not only watch the stock market, but also purchase and sell stocks all with the click of a button.


Is Tradiction free?

Tradiction's services are completely free to use, but the purchasing of stocks will be reliant on the users' bank account.


Is this website secure?

Yes, our website is secure. All confidential information is encrypted before being saved in our database.


How does the application make predictions?

Our application makes predictions through user posts on Twitter. The algorithm we use has been engineered to compile the data from Twitter and accurately make predictions on when to buy or sell stock.


My account status is still awaiting verification, how should I proceed?

Account verification takes about 2 days. If the status of your account has still not been resolved within that timeframe, please send a ticket using the "Contact Us" button.
